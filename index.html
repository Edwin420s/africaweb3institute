Who We Are

Africa Web3 Institute (AWI) sits at the intersection of technology, policy, and society. We translate complex emerging technologies into clear policy insights, practical education, and actionable frameworks for Africa's digital economy. From regulators to builders, from classrooms to cabinet rooms - we shape conversations that matter.

What We Do

Policy

We provide regulatory analysis, digital asset policy advisory, and stakeholder engagement services to governments and institutions across 15+ African countries.

Education

We run policy bootcamps, developer workshops, and executive training programs for regulators and Web3 founders across Africa.

Research

We publish quarterly research reports, policy briefs, and regulatory landscape analysis that informs innovation and governance across the continent.

Incubation

We support early-stage Web3 startups with mentorship, policy guidance, and access to our network of 500+ policymakers and industry leaders across Africa.

Africa is not catching up - it is building forward.

Web3 presents a once-in-a-generation opportunity to redesign financial systems, governance models, and digital ownership on African terms. We exist to ensure Africa leads responsibly, inclusively, and intelligently.
